ℹ️ Critical Point: Advertising Rules in Ontario

This is the most important factor for our Google Ads strategy. The Alcohol and Gaming Commission of Ontario (AGCO) imposes very strict rules on advertising "inducements."

What this means in practice: It is prohibited to advertise specific bonus offers (e.g., "100 Free Spins" or "$500 Deposit Bonus") in open communication channels like Google Ads. This information can only be displayed on the affiliate's or casino's own website after the user gives explicit consent to view it.

Implications for Our Strategy

Our ad copy cannot mention bonuses. We must focus our message on other differentiators:

  • Game Selection: "Over 1,500 Slots and Table Games."
  • Safety and Trust: "Only Licensed Casinos in Ontario."
  • Reviews and Analysis: "Honest Reviews. Find the Best Casino."
  • Game Categories: "Play the Best Live Dealer Games in Ontario."

Solution: The "Consent Gate"

The solution is to implement a "consent gate" or "age gate" on our landing page.

The user clicks on your Google ad.
They land on your URL, and a pop-up or screen overlay appears immediately.
The pop-up asks for age verification (19+) and consent to view promotional content.
Only after the user clicks "Accept & Enter" is the landing page with bonus offers revealed.
